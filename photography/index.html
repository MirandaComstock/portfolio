Photography

In some ways, I see my interest in photography as an extension of film, although it’s one moment frozen in time rather than a series of them. However, I only really got into photography when I started working on my high school’s yearbook, buying my first camera and taking photos of sports games and school events. Since then, I’ve loved to carry my camera with me almost everywhere. I’ve taken every photography course possible at UC Davis, and still love looking back on early photos I’ve taken.
